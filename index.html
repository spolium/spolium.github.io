  1. Yannick Nepomuk Fritz

I am a PhD Candidate at the University of Basel in the NOMIS project  «Assisted Thinking: A Deep History of Scholarly AI» where I work on a PhD project on prompting with AI models. I completed my M.A. in media philosophy and art research at Karlsruhe University of Arts and Design (HfG Karlsruhe) with a thesis on the political epistemology of generativity pertaining to artificial intelligence. Previously, I studied media and cultural studies in Vienna, Potsdam and Hong Kong.
